FAQ's

Know us better!

What is HashCards? HashCards is a crypto-based gifting platform that allows users to send and redeem secure, blockchain-backed gift cards.
How does it work? Once purchased, Gift card is minted as NFT and sent as a physical gift card having a protection pin, holding balances linked to blockchain addresses. Redeem or add balance using your wallet and the card’s unique codes.
How do I redeem a HashCard? Navigate to the redeem section, connect your wallet, and enter the token ID, physical code, NFC code, and PIN to claim the card’s balance.
